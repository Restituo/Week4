Crystal Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystal colors below. By clicking on a crystal, your score will go up a random value.

You win the game if you get the same total as the target score.

You lose the game if you go over the target score

The value of the crystal is hidden until you clicked on it.

For each game, the crystals are assigned a random value.
